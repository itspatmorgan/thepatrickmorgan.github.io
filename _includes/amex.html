My corner of the Amex universe revolved around the prepaid debit card platform, Serve. My team was responsible for the logged-in web experience of three brands on this platform: Amex Serve, Walmart Bluebird and Target Redcard. These three products are full-featured banking alternatives similar to the account you might have with a traditional bank like Chase or Wells Fargo (including bill pay, direct deposit, savings etc...).
