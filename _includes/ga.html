General Assembly

Web Development Immersive Student

After two years as a brand strategist at Leo Burnett in Chicago, I realized I wasn't happy stopping my work at the point of deciding the strategy; I wanted to be responsible for its execution. Web development became my path toward satisfying my fundamental need to make things. So I quit my job, moved to New York and put myself through the ringer at General Assembly before finding my niche at Amex.

Please note: These are projects from my earliest days as a developer. They're fun to browse, but I've obviously learned a lot in the 2+ years since making them.
