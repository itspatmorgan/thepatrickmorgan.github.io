At Vroozi I'm the only full-time designer, so I'm responsible for and do pretty much everything design-wise. I really do a bit of everything in this role: I work with my product team partners to gather requirements, I might lead us in experience design and research activities, I might create an InVision prototype, I might do visual design exploration, I might create a content matrix for a new feature, or I might just hop into the code base to fix a bug. Ultimately, I do what's needed to communicate the design intent.
